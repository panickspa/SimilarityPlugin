About Plugin¶

Plugin created by Pandu Wicaksono and Takdir. There is no external library included in this plugin except PyQGIS. Similarity score implementing GOF MapCurve method (Hargrove et. al. (2006) doi:10.1007/s10109-006-0025-x ). Further information you can contact us at email panickspa@gmail.com.
